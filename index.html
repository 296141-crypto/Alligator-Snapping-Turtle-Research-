How Do We Know What We Know About The Alligator Snapping Turtle?

The question that had guided my research project was "How Do We Know What We Know About The Alligator snapping Turtle?". This question is pretty much asking what tools and methods of research are used to gather information on these animals. The question associates well with the animal because the AST is very uncommon and quite hard to find. My research process consisted of simple google searches and the use of AI. Both of these methods linked me to highly informational articles on my topic.

After reading the articles, I would copy the most important and specific details onto a note document on google docs. This strategy helped me save key ideas on the topic which granted better knowledge to be able to make a site like this. Over the many days of research, I learned many different facts on how we understand factors of the AST. The most commonly used way to catch these elusive animals is by putting dead fish in a hoop net and waiting for days. As for getting measurements, most researchers use measuring tapes for shell size and large scales for weight.
